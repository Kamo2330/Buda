How It Works

Simple steps to find your perfect home or list your property

For Tenants

Find your perfect home in 3 simple steps

1
Search & Discover

Use our advanced search filters to find properties that match your needs, budget, and location preferences. Browse listings with photos and host-provided details.

  • Advanced filters
  • Map view
  • Host-listed properties
  • Detailed photos
2
Apply or Book

Apply for monthly rentals or book instantly for short stays. Connect with hosts through Qasha messaging before you book or apply.

  • Instant booking
  • Secure messaging
  • Application tracking
  • Optional host badges
3
Move In

Inspect the property, confirm details with the host, and use in-app Help if you need support.

  • Secure payments
  • Help when you need it
  • Move-in assistance
  • Pay on Qasha (optional)

For Hosts

List your property and start earning in 3 simple steps

1
Create Your Listing

Upload photos, add property details, set pricing, and describe your space. Our step-by-step process makes it easy to create an attractive listing.

  • Photo upload
  • Property details
  • Pricing setup
  • Amenities selection
2
Optional verification badge

Hosts may apply for a paid Verified Landlord or Agency badge after document review. This is not a property inspection or background check.

  • Document review
  • Admin approval
  • Trust badge on profile
  • See Terms for limits
3
Start Earning

Your listing goes live after you publish. You receive messages and booking requests from registered users.

  • Instant bookings
  • Secure payments
  • Booking requests
  • Help when you need it
