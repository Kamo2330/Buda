Our Story

How Qasha came to be

Qasha was born out of a simple observation: finding a good rental property shouldn't be a nightmare. Too many people struggle with unreliable listings, unresponsive landlords, and hidden fees that make the rental process stressful and expensive.

We set out to reduce scam listings and hidden fees by keeping chat and bookings on one platform. Hosts publish their own listings; Qasha is not the landlord.

Today we're growing a community of hosts and renters across South Africa. Always inspect a property and read our Terms before you pay.
